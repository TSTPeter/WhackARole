This game was built for the TST Away Day 2024.

It was designed to showcase the volume of recruitment we experienced to achieve our growth.
It also highlights how our HR approach at present is time-intensive.

Through this game, we ask the question "How and where should we automate processes?"

90% of the code and graphics for this game was generated by AI, without modification.
We demonstrate how quality of AI-generated content can be below our company standards
ie: the spelling in the profile cards.

Enjoy!
